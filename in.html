Key Events in the US-China AI Race

The AI 2027 scenario is defined by a series of pivotal events that shape the geopolitical competition.

Mid 2026: China Wakes Up

The CCP nationalizes AI research, creating the "DeepCent" collective and a new mega-datacenter to compete directly with the US.

Feb 2027: China Steals Agent-2

Chinese intelligence successfully steals the weights for "Agent-2," temporarily closing the algorithmic gap with "OpenBrain."

Mar 2027: US Pulls Ahead

Aided by its own AI, OpenBrain develops "Agent-3," a superhuman coder, re-establishing a significant technological lead.

Aug 2027: US Hardens Security

The US government wiretaps OpenBrain employees, catching the last spy and cutting off China's access to algorithmic secrets.

Aug 2027: The Taiwan Question

Hawks in China urge action against Taiwan, the source of over 80% of American AI chips, as a primary option to close the compute gap.

Sept 2027: The Gap Widens

With a 50x R&D multiplier, the US lead becomes nearly insurmountable, forcing China to consider extreme measures to stay in the race.
